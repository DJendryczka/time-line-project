The Time Line

"In the beginning... well there really was no 'beginning' as time itself did not exist. The Creator existed in something similar to a continuum,, without a clear start or end. However, at a certain point, the Creator wished to share the joy derived from existence, the potential for life, and the ability to create one's own identity. It was then that what we perceive as the 'beginning' commenced. Thus, our universe came into being, accompanied by the passage of time."

Date 0.0.0

Time begins with the creation of the first being similar to the Creator. (John 1:1-3) "In the beginning was the Word, and the Word was with God, and the Word was a god. This one was in the beginning with God. All things came into existence through him, and apart from him not even one thing came into existence."

Proverbs 8: 22-31 in verse 22, wisdom says: “Jehovah produced me as the beginning of his way, the earliest of his achievements of long ago.” More than just wisdom must be involved here, for that quality never was “produced.” It never began to exist because Jehovah has always existed and he has always been wise. (Psalm 90:2) God’s Son, however, was “the firstborn of all creation.” He was produced, or created; he was the earliest of all of Jehovah’s achievements. (Colossians 1:15) The Son existed before the earth and the heavens, as described in Proverbs. And as the Word, God’s own Spokesman, he was the perfect expression of Jehovah’s wisdom.​ John 1:1.
